Undergraduate Library

Services

Group and Individual Study

Take advantage of reservable Group Rooms, large tables, and technology-enhanced spaces on the Upper Level to work with friends and classmates, and quiet study spaces on the lower level for individual work.

Collections

Explore our broad combination of traditional textbook and background information materials and unique popular culture items, including genre fiction, graphic novels, feature films, TV series, and video games.

Technology

Enhance your projects with hardware from our Loanable Technology (cameras, lighting, audio recorders, etc.) and high-end computing resources from our Media Commons.

Expert Assistance

Improve your skills by visiting with our variety of consultation services, including Librarian Office Hours, the Writer's Workshop, Media Commons Technology Specialists, and Resume and Cover Letter critiques.
